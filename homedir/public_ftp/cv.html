Skills

Concept

Generating ideas and concepts is a daily, satisfying process of my life. Often while running or during my usual afternoon nap idea flourish with ease. Ideas are then evaluated and possibly implemented.

Web

My understanding of web design has matured over the year from a mild interest to a beneficial obsession. I now possess a solid understanding of responsive design, image optimization, SEO, color theory, speed and performance.

UX

Creating a consistent and fluid user experience is key to my design. Every digital product has its own story. Research, strategy, information architecture, content, CTA, user flows  through to testing are all key aspects to investigate.  

UI

The UX research phase is then translated into elegant user interface that balance typography, color, animation and cognitive load. The visual hierarchies required for the UI must stem from user requirements.

Copyright

Bio

Originally from Florence, trained in London, living in Berlin, working everywhere. A digital nomad of sorts.

Formally educated as an interaction designer I now craft websites, create interactive installations, write and try to exercise daily with arguable results. 

When I'm not dealing with pixels, motors, lasers or valves I craft handmade caps. I make caps to get ready for the impending baldness and to personally support and contribute to craftsmanship in a time of mass-produced mediocrity.
Society deserves better products; better stories.

I believe that happiness is to be found in the process of getting to something, not the actual thing. As such I am constantly submerged in multiple creative projects around the world. Sometimes for $, some other time for fun, virtue or ethic integrity.

In September 2013 I stopped flying for environmental reasons. If that sort of thing floats your boat you can read about it here.
